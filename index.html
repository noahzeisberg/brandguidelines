Brand Guidelines

Our primary brand typeface

Outfit

Outfit should be used in most normal cases. If Outfit doesn't fit, consider using another FontKit typeface or the default font. As weights, every weight from light to bold is considered as suitable for designing. Fonts weighting lower or higher are considered as unreadable or unsettling to read and should be avoided.

Primary

Our Logomark

Our Logomark is the primary piece of identification of our brand. It should be used in nearly all cases if possible, especially where a small piece of brand identity is needed or few horizontal space is available.

Secondary

Our combined logo

Our combined logo is the secondary piece of identification of our brand. The combined logo should be used in cases where horizontal space is unlimited, but is forbidden to be used on small screens or views, even if enough horizontal space is available. It also shouldn't be used in minimalistic applications, which is where you should use the primary logomark.
