Brand Guidelines

Our primary brand typeface

Outfit

Outfit should be used in most normal cases. If Outfit doesn't fit, consider using another FontKit typeface or the default font. As weights, every weight from light to bold is considered as suitable for designing. Fonts weighting lower or higher are considered as unreadable or unsettling to read and should be avoided.
